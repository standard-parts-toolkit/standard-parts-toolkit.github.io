## The challenge

[Northbound Truck & Off-Road] had a 50,000-SKU catalog on Shopify with no fitment search. Shoppers could see every brake pad in the store, but couldn't narrow to ones that fit their truck. Conversion was suffering, and the team had explored multiple "YMM plugins" — every one of them assumed the merchant had ACES/PIES data, which [Northbound] didn't.

## The solution

[Northbound] uploaded a CSV they were already maintaining in a spreadsheet. SPT's CSV connector handled column mapping, validated the file, and synced fitment to Shopify metafields. YMM Search dropped into the theme as a Shopify block, styled to match the existing brand without theme customization.

## The result

YMM Search was live across all 50,000 SKUs in under two weeks. Shoppers could now narrow to vehicle-specific results, the "no fit" warning kept them from buying the wrong part, and the team didn't need engineering support to maintain it. [Stats placeholder — conversion lift, returns reduction.]
