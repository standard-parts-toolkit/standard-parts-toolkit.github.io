## The challenge

[Acme Performance Parts] had a mature catalog in ACES/PIES, syndicated to dozens of resellers — but no direct-to-consumer presence. Every previous attempt to launch their own storefront ran into the same wall: turning ACES fitment and PIES product information into something Shopify could render meant custom development that took quarters and broke on every theme update.

## The solution

[Acme] connected SPT to their existing ACES/PIES drops. Within days, Catalog Sync had rebuilt the entire catalog as Shopify products and metafields, YMM Search was wired into the theme, and PDP Enrichment was rendering authoritative fitment tables and Buyer's Guide content on every product page. In-Cart Fitment captured the shopper's vehicle through checkout, and Order Export pushed paid orders to [Acme]'s existing fulfillment system in the format their warehouse already expected.
